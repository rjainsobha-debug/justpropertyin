Privacy Policy

This is a basic launch privacy policy page for JustProperty.in. Please have it reviewed by your legal advisor before using it for live lead campaigns.

Information we may collect

When you submit an enquiry through JustProperty.in, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, preferred location, budget and property requirement details.

We may use your information to respond to your enquiry, provide property options, contact you regarding relevant real estate opportunities and improve our services.

Information sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share relevant enquiry details with our internal team, authorised representatives, channel partners or builders only where reasonably required to serve your request.

Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect your submitted information, but no website or transmission method can be guaranteed to be fully secure.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact info@justproperty.in.
